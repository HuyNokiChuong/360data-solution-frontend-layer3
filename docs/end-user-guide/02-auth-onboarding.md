# 02. Dang Nhap Va Onboarding

## 1. Muc tieu module
- Dang nhap vao workspace.
- Hoan tat thong tin ho so ban dau.
- Xac thuc email bang ma OTP de kich hoat tai khoan.

## 2. Dieu kien tien quyet
- Email cong ty hop le.
- Mat khau hop le do he thong cap/tao.

## 3. Quy trinh thao tac
### 3.1 Dang nhap
1. Mo man hinh `System Access`.
2. Nhap `Corporate Identity (Work Email)`.
3. Nhap `Access Token (Password)`.
4. Bam `Enter Workspace`.

### 3.2 Dang ky workspace moi
1. Bam `Create Account`.
2. Nhap thong tin ca nhan/bo phan/cong ty.
3. Gui dang ky.
4. He thong chuyen sang man `Onboarding`.

### 3.3 Onboarding 4 buoc
1. Chon vai tro cong viec (`Job Title`).
2. Chon quy mo cong ty (`Company Size`).
3. Nhap so dien thoai lien he.
4. Nhap ma xac thuc 6 so gui qua email.

### 3.4 Gui lai ma xac thuc
1. Cho countdown ve `0`.
2. Bam `Resend Code`.
3. Kiem tra mailbox va nhap ma moi.

## 4. Dau hieu thanh cong
- User chuyen vao trang `Connections`.
- Trang thai user chuyen sang `Active`.

## 5. Loi thuong gap va cach xu ly
- "Access restricted: Please use your corporate email.": dung email domain cong ty.
- "Invalid verification code": kiem tra lai ma OTP 6 so moi nhat.
- Khong nhan ma OTP: kiem tra spam/junk, sau do bam resend.
